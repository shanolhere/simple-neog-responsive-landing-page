Why this course?

  • Beginner-Friendly

    You have never written a single line of code. No problem, we got you covered. Start with levelZero. levelZero of neoG Camp is absolutely beginner-friendly.

  • A-Z of Fullstack Web Dev

    After this bootcamp, you’ll be able to create and host an app end to end. No problem, we got you covered. This structured camp will take you from a beginner to an advanced level programmer.

  • Project-Based Learning

    You will learn everything through live project. You will create more than 20 projects during this journey. Projects which are fun to share with your friends and impressive to your future employer as well.

  • Dedicated Peer Groups

    You will have a closed and dedicated classroomexperience. You will have this extraordinary alumni group as your biggest asset for career building. Make friends, network, find your future colleagues, cofounders, and referrals here.

  • 100+ Hours of Live Teaching

    No boring recorded videosto learn alone. You will learn to live with your peers, ask doubts and discuss with your mentor and others. You will also have your team to discuss lectures and network.

  • Interview Readiness

    You become industry-ready with us. We have a few sessions reserved for interview preparation. Everything taught to you is industry-relevant and job-oriented too.
